What if?

Description

The Prompt of this project was to create a story revolving around a concept we chose from a list of projects and collect images in relation to the concept as well.

Tools

Google Doc, Image Search

Process Documentation

This project was alot more minimal compared to other projects of the semester.

Reflection

While working on this project I found alot of interest in the open-ended style of the prompt as well as the creative writing aspect of the project too. The concept I based my project on was around folklore and I specifically focused on the fresno night crawler cryptid for my design. Finding images related to the creature I had chosen did prove to be somewhat difficult as only a few snapshots of the creature exist and I had to really dig around to find what I felt was an appropriate representation of the cryptid. Beyond the image search however the writing portion was quite fun as I based it roughly around my own job that I work as a janitor at AMC.

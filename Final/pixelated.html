Pixelated

Description

The prompt for this project was to find four images of interest and then pixelate them to different degrees by resizing the image.

Tools

Adobe Photoshop

Process Documentation

Errors encountered - miss understanding of size expectation for documents.

Reflection

While working on this project I didn't have too much trouble sourcing my images. I decided to look for interesting patterns found in nature and then further narrowed down my search to fish in particular for their vibrant patterns since I felt they would read well as they were sized down and pixelated. I was familiar with some of the basic photoshop tools and easily navigated the project however I did misread the size expectations for the project which further affected how my final project turned out. Otherwise it seemed somewhat straight forward and I was very interested and seeing what would emerge from the smaller more pixilated patterns.
